About Brag Document Generator

What is a Brag Document?

A brag document is a comprehensive record of your professional accomplishments, learning experiences, and skills utilized over a specific period. It serves as a valuable tool for performance reviews, career development discussions, and personal reflection.

How It Works

  1. Upload Your Data: Upload an Excel file containing your daily scrum reports or work logs
  2. AI Processing: Our AI analyzes your data using advanced language models (Gemini or Groq)
  3. Structured Output: Get a professionally formatted brag document with organized sections
  4. Export & Share: Download as Markdown or view online for easy sharing

Document Structure

Each generated brag document includes:

  • Work Accomplishments: Goals achieved, project milestones, and personal reflections
  • Learning: New skills acquired, technologies learned, and knowledge gained
  • Utilized Skills: Technical and soft skills demonstrated during the period

Technology Stack

Built with modern technologies for reliability and performance:

  • Backend: Django 5.2 with Python
  • AI Integration: LangChain with Gemini and Groq APIs
  • Database: PostgreSQL
  • Frontend: Responsive HTML/CSS/JavaScript

Privacy & Security

Your data privacy is our priority:

  • Uploaded files are processed temporarily and automatically deleted
  • Generated documents are stored securely and only accessible to you
  • API keys are used only for document generation and not stored
